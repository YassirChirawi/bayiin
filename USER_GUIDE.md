## ğŸ’° Finances & Profit

Stop guessing your numbers. We calculate them for you.

### **The Formula**
We track your **Real Cash**:
> **Profit** = (Sold Price) - (Product Cost) - (Shipping Fees) - (Ads & Expenses)

### **Adding Expenses**
Did you spend money on Facebook Ads or Packaging?
1. Go to **Finances**.
2. Under "Expenses Management", verify the category (e.g., "Ads").
3. Enter the amount and description.
4. Click **Add**.
   *This instantly updates your Net Profit calculation!*
